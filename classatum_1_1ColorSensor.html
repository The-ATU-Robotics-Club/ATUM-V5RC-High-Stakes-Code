atum::ColorSensor::ColorSensor ( const std::int8_t  port,
const std::vector< HueField >  iHueFields,
const Logger::Level  loggerLevel = Logger::Level::Info 
)

Constructs a new ColorSensor with the given port. Hue fields given should form a partition (i.e., they shouldn't overlap; think of centers and threshold as "angles"), otherwise will choose color based on order given when in the threshold for multiple fields.

Also turns on LED to max brightness and disables gestures.

By providing a port, reconnecting is supported whenever the device isn't connected at the beginning of the match.
