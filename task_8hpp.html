class  atum::Task
 This class allows its derivatives to "own" their own tasks and better control their management. To use, publicly derive on the desired class and use the TASK_BOILERPLATE macro. Then, in the cpp file for the class, begin task definitions with "TASK_DEFINITIONS_FOR(<name of the class>) {", "START_TASK(<name>, <priority (optional>))" to begin defining a task, and "END_TASK" to cap the task definition.
